What you'll need:
  • A festive wreath
  • Micro lights (we suggest a string of 50 LEDs)

  1. Tuck one end of your micro lights into the foliage and secure by twisting it around the frame of the wreath - the cable is super bendy so no additional wire is needed to fasten it in place.
  2. In a spiral motion, loosely wrap the lights around the garland by weaving them through the centre in a circular motion.
  3. Once you've gone around the entire wreath, tightly tuck the end of the lights into the foliage, ensuring that there's enough cable left so that you can hide the battery box out of sight.
  4. Hang your wreath in the perfect spot, turn on the lights and voila you have your sparkly wreath! Opt for fairy lights with a timer for the ultimate low maintenance Christmas light.

What you'll need:
  • Plain candles
  • Glitter, colour of your choice
  • PVA glue
  • Crafting Brush
  • Sealer spray, clear
  • Masking tape, 1inch width
Time: Less than 30 minutes!
  1. Mark off the area you want ot glitter on the candle using the masking tape.
  2. Using a crafting brush, paint the PVA glue onto the marked off area.
  3. Sprinkle glitter over the candle until all of the glue is covered.
  4. Let the candles dry overnight. Then on the next day give them a light spray using the Sealer spray to help keep the glitter from flaking off later.
